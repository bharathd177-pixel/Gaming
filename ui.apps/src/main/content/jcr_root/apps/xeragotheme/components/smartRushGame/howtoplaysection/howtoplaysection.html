How to Play

  • Swipe left, right, up, or down to move your avatar along the coloured train lines.
  • Collect the grocery, transport, online shopping, and many more icons placed along the coloured train lines.
  • Change directions at the junctions to collect the icons.
  • Each icon = 5 points
  • Score 100 points within 1 minute to win a reward.
  • Keep an eye on the timer! ⏱
